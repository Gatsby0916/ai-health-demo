1. Input

Users describe symptoms, daily routines, stress context, and lifestyle constraints in free text.

2. Prompted AI Analysis

The backend sends the text to a controlled prompt designed for non-medical lifestyle advice and risk-aware wording.

3. Structured Response

The output is formatted into actionable sections, including practical next steps and safety reminders.

4. Safety Layer

The assistant explicitly avoids diagnosis and drug recommendations, and asks users to seek medical help when high-risk signals are present.
